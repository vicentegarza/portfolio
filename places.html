PLACES

I am from Monterrey, Mexico and I have lived here a good part of my life. However, I lived a few years in the USA: first a few months during my high school senior year and then several years during my post-graduate studies. Here's a little bit of information about Monterrey.

Monterrey

The Monterrey metropolitan area is the second largest in Mexico. It has a population of over 5 million people. The metropolitan area includes several cities besides the city of Monterrey.
